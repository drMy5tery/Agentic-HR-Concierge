# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
This code sets out the standards of behaviour expected of everyone at the
company. It applies in the workplace, at company events, and in all
work-related communication.

## Expected Behaviour
Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism. Act
with honesty and integrity, follow lawful instructions, and protect company and
customer information.

## Anti-Harassment and Anti-Discrimination
The company does not tolerate harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any
kind, including on the basis of gender, age, religion, caste, disability, or
sexual orientation. Sexual harassment is prohibited and is dealt with under the
Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) framework and the Internal Committee.

## Reporting Concerns
Concerns about harassment, discrimination, or other misconduct are treated as
sensitive and **confidential**. They are handled by the People Operations team
and, where applicable, the Internal Committee — not through ordinary
self-service channels. Raising a concern in good faith will never result in
retaliation. If you are unsure how to proceed, ask HR to route your concern to
People Operations confidentially.

## Conflicts of Interest
Declare any personal, financial, or family interest that could conflict with
your work. Do not accept gifts or hospitality that could improperly influence a
business decision.

## Confidentiality
Keep confidential information secure and use it only for legitimate business
purposes. This obligation continues after employment ends.

## Disciplinary Action
Breaches of this code may lead to disciplinary action up to and including
termination, and, where relevant, referral to the authorities.
