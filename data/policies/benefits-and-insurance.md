# Benefits & Insurance Policy

## Overview
This policy summarises the health, insurance, and savings benefits available to
full-time employees. Benefits begin on your date of joining unless stated
otherwise.

## Group Medical Insurance
All full-time employees are enrolled in the company group medical insurance
scheme from their joining date. The policy covers hospitalisation, day-care
procedures, and pre- and post-hospitalisation expenses as defined by the
insurer.

## Coverage and Dependents
The base policy covers the employee, their spouse, and up to two dependent
children. Parents may be added during the annual enrolment window for an
additional premium deducted from salary.

## Sum Insured
The default family floater sum insured is INR 5,00,000 per year. Employees may
top up to INR 10,00,000 during annual enrolment at their own cost.

## Claims Process
Cashless treatment is available at network hospitals using your e-card.
For non-network hospitals, pay upfront and submit a reimbursement claim through
the insurer's portal within 30 days of discharge. HR can raise a ticket to help
track a claim, but the insurer adjudicates all claims.

## Life and Accident Cover
Employees are covered by group term life insurance equal to three times annual
basic salary, and by a group personal accident policy covering accidental death
and permanent disability.

## Wellness Programme
The wellness programme provides an annual health check-up, access to a
tele-consultation service, and an employee assistance programme (EAP) offering
confidential counselling.

## Provident Fund
Employees and the company each contribute 12% of basic salary to the Employees'
Provident Fund (EPF). Contributions and the current balance are visible on the
EPFO member portal.
