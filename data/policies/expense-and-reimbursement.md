# Expense & Reimbursement Policy

## Overview
This policy explains which work-related expenses can be claimed, the limits that
apply, and how to submit a claim for reimbursement.

## Eligible Expenses
Reimbursable expenses include approved business travel, accommodation, client
meals, and pre-approved professional development such as courses and
certifications. Expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and incurred for
legitimate business purposes.

## Submission Process
Submit claims through the Keka expense module with an itemised receipt for each
expense. Claims without a valid receipt may be rejected. Group related expenses
under a single claim for the same trip or event where possible.

## Limits and Approval
- Daily meal allowance on business travel: up to INR 1,200 per day.
- Domestic accommodation: up to INR 5,000 per night.
- Any single expense above INR 20,000 requires prior written manager approval.

## Timelines
Submit expense claims within 30 days of the date the expense was incurred.
Approved claims are reimbursed with the next payroll cycle. Claims older than 60
days may be rejected.

## Non-Reimbursable Items
Personal expenses, fines and penalties, alcohol (except at approved client
events), and travel upgrades paid for personal preference are not reimbursable.
