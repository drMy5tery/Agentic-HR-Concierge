# Remote Work Policy

## Overview
This policy describes how hybrid and remote working operate, who is eligible,
and the expectations that apply when working away from the office.

## Eligibility
Hybrid working is available to employees whose roles can be performed
effectively away from the office, subject to manager agreement. Some roles that
require on-site presence are not eligible.

## Work-From-Home Days
Eligible employees may work from home up to three days per week. The specific
days are agreed with your manager and your team to ensure adequate on-site
overlap.

## Equipment and Stipend
The company provides a laptop and necessary software. Employees working in a
hybrid arrangement receive a one-time home-office setup stipend of INR 15,000
and a monthly internet allowance of INR 1,000.

## Availability and Core Hours
While working remotely you are expected to be reachable during core hours of
11:00 to 17:00 and to attend scheduled meetings. Update your calendar and
status to reflect your availability.

## Security Expectations
Use the company VPN for access to internal systems, keep your device locked when
unattended, and never store customer data on personal devices or unapproved
cloud services. Report any lost device or suspected security incident to IT
immediately.
